Travel to Bora Bora

Welcome to paradise on earth. Bora Bora is an island in French Polynesia, surrounded by a lagoon and a barrier reef. It is the perfect place to relax under the palm trees.

Things to see

  • Mount Otemanu — the peak of a dormant volcano.
  • Matira Beach — a famous white sand beach.
  • Lagoonarium — a natural aquarium with fish.

Things to do

  1. Stay in a bungalow standing right over the water.
  2. Snorkel among the corals.
  3. Try Tahitian fruits.
