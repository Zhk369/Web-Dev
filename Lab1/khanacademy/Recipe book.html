Recipe #1: Air-fryer jacket potato

  • Time: 41 mins
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
baking potatoes 2 (250g)
olive oil 25g

Step 1: Use a sharp knife to prick the potatoes a few times, then rub each potato with 1 teaspoon of olive oil and sprinkle with a small pinch of sea salt.

Step 2: Place the potatoes in the air fryer* in a single layer and cook at 180°C for 40 minutes or until soft, turning halfway if your air fryer has a basket rather than shelves.

Step 3: Serve with your favourite toppings – see our inspo below!

Recipe #2: Smoked trout pâté

  • Time: 5 mins
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Cream cheese 300g
Jarred horseradish 3 heaped teaspoons
Large lemon (plus extra wedges) 1
Fresh chives ½ a bunch (10g)
Hot-smoked trout (skin/bones removed) 300g
Extra virgin olive oil To taste / As needed

Step 1: Place the cream cheese and horseradish in a large bowl. Finely grate in the zest of ½ the lemon, then squeeze in all of the juice. Finely chop and stir in most of the chives, then flake in the fish in nice chunks.

Step 2: Use a spatula to fold the mixture together gently. Taste and season to perfection with sea salt and black pepper, then add 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil – you want it to have a bit of attitude, with a good balance of spicy, smoky and salty flavours, so add a little more horseradish or lemon juice, if you think it needs it.

Step 3: Sprinkle over the remaining chives and finish with an extra drizzle of oil, if you like. Cover and place in the fridge until needed.

Step 4: Transfer the pâté to a nice bowl, and serve with hunks of crusty bread and lemon wedges, for squeezing over.

Recipe #3: Black bean taco filling

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Garlic 1 clove
Olive oil To taste / As needed
Black beans 1 x 400g tin
Smoked paprika 1 teaspoon
Mixed salad leaves (spinach, watercress, rocket) 50g

Step 1: Peel and finely slice the garlic, then place in a large frying pan on a medium-high heat with 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Fry for 1 minute, then pour in the beans (juice and all) and stir in the smoked paprika.

Step 2: Let it bubble down to a thick sauce, then stir through the salad leaves until wilted and season to perfection with sea salt and black pepper.

Step 3: Serve on your favourite taco shells with tomato salsa, charred tinned sweetcorn and a squeeze of lime juice.
